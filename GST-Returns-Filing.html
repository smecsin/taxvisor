    What is GST Return?


    GST Returns are the statement of information furnished by the taxpayer to GST Department at regular intervals. The information to be furnished in the return generally comprises of the details of Purchases, Sales, Output GST (on Sales) and Input Tax Credit (GST paid on purchases). Our experts will file your GST Returns. The taxpayer is generally required to furnish the return in specific format given by GST Department

    Every registered person in GST has to mandatorily file their GST returns irrespective of sales/purchases made on timely basis.

    If your turnover is more than 20 lakh/10lakh*(special category states) then you need to get registered and file returns depending upon your registration


    For Regular Dealer

    GSTR 1 For Outward Supply by business
    GSTR 2 For Purchases made by business
    GSTR 3 For Assessing of liability and Payment of Tax
    GSTR 3B For Outward supply, purchases made, assessing liability and payment of taxes. (Monthly).
    GSTR 9 Required to be filed annually


    All returns have to mandatorily filed even if no outward supply, purchases occurred during a month.

    GST Invoice content:

    A tax invoice is generally issued to charge the tax and pass on the input tax credit to purchaser. A GST compliant tax invoice is a bill which will have:

    • Name, address and GSTIN of the supplier
    • Invoice number
    • Date of issue
    • Name, address and GSTIN of the recipient (if registered)
    • HSN code
    • Description of the goods/services
    • Quantity of goods
    • Value after discount
    • Rate and amount of GST
