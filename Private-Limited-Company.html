    Eligibilty

    Minimum Requirements to form a Private Limited Company

    i. Minimum 2 (Two) Persons & Maximum 200 Persons.
    ii. No Minimum Capital Required
    iii. One Resident Director.
    iv. Name of the Company should be Unique

    Minimum Requirements to form a Public Limited Company

    v. Minimum capital requirement for a public limited company is Rs. 5, 00,000
    vi. Minimum number of members or shareholders required is 7 (seven)
    vii. Must have minimum number of at least 3 directors – The Director needs to be over 18 years of age and must be a natural person. There are no limitations in terms of citizenship or residency.

    Frequently Asked Question

    What is private company?
    A company that is owned by one person or a small group of people, for example a family, and whose shares are not traded on a stock market,” or “a company that is not owned by the government.
    What is eligibility criteria to start a private and public limited company?
    Minimum Requirements to form a Private Limited Company
    i. Minimum of two shareholders are required.
    ii. A minimum of two shareholders and a maximum of up to 200 shareholders are allowed in a private limited company. The shareholders could be natural persons or companies, including foreign companies.
    iii. Minimum of two Directors and can have up to a maximum of fifteen Directors.
    iv. Minimum authorized share capital is Rs. 1,00,000. There is no limit specified for minimum paid capital.

    Minimum Requirements to form a Public Limited Company
    i. The capital requirement for a public limited company is Rs. 5, 00,000
    ii. Minimum number of members or shareholders required is 7 (seven)
    iii. Must have minimum number of at least 3 directors – The Director needs to be over 18 years of age and must be a natural person. There are no limitations in terms of citizenship or residency.

    What is the Minimum capital requirement to start a company?
    There is no requirement of minimum capital, as the government has done away with the requirement of having the minimum share capital. Now any person can form a private limited company with any amount as per his choice.
    What is a DSC & DIN?
    The DSC is an electronic device which can be used to sign electronic documents. DIN is a director identification number, for registering a company at least two proposed directors should have a valid DIN
    What is Articles of Association (AOA) and Memorandum of Association (MOA) ?
    AOA refers to articles of association which defines the internal constitution of the company, and MOA defines mission, vision, business objectives of the company in the log run.
    What are the relaxation provided by the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) w.e.f 26th January 2018 ?
    Here are key highlights of the changes made by the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) w.e.f 26thJanuary 2018:
    • Fees for final submission of company registration form have been reduced.
    • The final form fee waiver is only for companies formed with authorized capital up to Rs.10 lakh.
    • There will be no need for DIN and DSC up to name approval process. Once the name is finalized, DIN can be applied directly via SPICE incorporation form.
    As said, there are many other small changes has been made, which you will observe once we discuss the procedure in detail
    What is RUN Services and how to reserve the name of the company through RUN services?
    A new service has been introduced by the mca for easy reservation of name and fast reservation of name.
    Applicant have to login into their account on MCA Website. (Pro-existing users can use earlier account or new users have to create a new account.)
    After Login use have to click on the icon “RUN” in MCA Service. An online form shall be open. Applicants have to fill the information online.
    Only one Name can be mentioned in RUN form.
    Only one Re submission of application is allowed in case of reservation of Name.
    DSC & DIN not required for filing of RUN form for reservation of Name.
    What shall be the validity of the Name after approval?
    i. Reserved name shall be valid for 20 days in case of allotment of name for New Company
    ii. Reserved name shall be valid for 60 days in case of allotment of name for existing Company (Change of Name).
    10. Within how many days a private company gets incorporated?
    We can incorporate a Private Limited Company in India from 8-9 days. The time taken for registration will depend on submission of relevant documents by the client and speed of Government Approvals.
    To ensure speedy registration, please choose a unique name for your Company and ensure you have all the required documents prior to starting the registration process

    Do I need to separately apply for pan and tan?
    No. As per the new incorporation policy, the MCA allot PAN and TAN along with the incorporation certificate.
    Can I convert private company to public company and vice versa?
    Yes
    Conversion by Default :- where if a Private or public Company made a default in complying with basic conditions prescribed in the definition of a Private or public Company, the company shall cease to be entitled to the privileges and exemptions conferred on private or public companies and vice versa.
    Voluntary Conversion:- The company can also voluntarily convert private to public and vice versa.
