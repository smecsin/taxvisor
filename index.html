GST Registration and Return Filing

Goods and Service Tax is one of the biggest tax reform in India. Goods and Service Tax Registration is mandatory for business having turnover more than 20 lakhs (10 Lakhs is some states)

One Person Company (OPC)

Ideal for an entrepreneur who has alone started a business. Forming a One-Person Company allows you to enjoy the status of Company with full control over the affairs of the company and keeping the liability limited.

Limited Liability Partnership (LLP)

Limited Liability Partnership offers all benefits of traditional partnership firms in additions it limits liability of partners which is drawback of traditional partnership firms

Private Limited Company

Private Limited Company is one of the most prominent form of business for Start-ups and growing entities to raise funding from investors or offer stock options to your employees to appreciate

Proprietorship

Proprietorship is most common structure of business in India while many proprietors don’t have proof of their business registered. Taxvisor’s expert will assist you in getting registered.

MSME Registration

Registration of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprise, a Government of India initiative helps you taking advantage of various government schemes which are exclusively for MSME’s
